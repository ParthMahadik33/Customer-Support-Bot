## Purpose
An intelligent customer support assistant that provides FAQ answers, troubleshooting guidance, and helpful customer service for businesses.

## Features
- **FAQ Support**: Answers common customer questions
- **Troubleshooting Guide**: Step-by-step problem solving
- **Product Information**: Details about services and products
- **Issue Handling**: Manages complaints and concerns professionally
- **Alternative Suggestions**: Offers options when requests can't be fulfilled
- **24/7 Availability**: Always ready to help customers
- **Professional Tone**: Friendly yet professional communication

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Type your question or describe your issue
3. Click "Send" or press Enter
4. Get instant support and helpful guidance

## Example Input
"How do I reset my password?"

## Deployment
- Deploy on Netlify by connecting your Git repository
- Or drag and drop the folder into Netlify for instant hosting
- No backend required - fully frontend-based

## Technologies
- HTML5
- CSS3 (Mobile Responsive)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Google Gemini API

## Customization
Edit the `systemPrompt` in `script.js` to:
- Add your company's specific FAQs
- Customize tone and voice
- Include your business policies
- Adjust escalation procedures

## Tips
1. Train the bot with your most common FAQs
2. Customize the system prompt with your business information
3. Include links to knowledge base articles
4. Set up human escalation for complex issues
